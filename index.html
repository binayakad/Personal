About Me

My name is Binayak Adhikari, and I am a computer engineer based Inaruwa. I have always been interested in technology, and after graduating from college with a degree in Computer Science, I pursued a career in computer science development.

Throughout my career, I have worked on various projects, ranging from network engineer to designing web applications. I have also had the opportunity to work with a diverse group of individuals, which has allowed me to develop my communication and problem-solving skills.

In addition to my work in software development, I am also passionate about giving back to the community. I volunteer at a local non-profit organization that provides computer training to underprivileged youth. Through this experience, I have been able to inspire and mentor young people to pursue careers in technology.

When I'm not working, I enjoy spending time outdoors, hiking, and exploring new places. I also love to read and stay up-to-date with the latest technology trends and innovations.

Overall, I am a driven individual who is always looking for new challenges and opportunities for growth. I am excited to see what the future holds, and I am committed to making a positive impact in the world through my work and my passion for technology.
